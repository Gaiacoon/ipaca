iPaca: learning academic english

With the start of their studies many people face new challenges. One of many is academic reading. iPaca tries to help you master academic reading by providing you a possibly to practice it every day. With these short sessions that you can easily fit into your day. This helps you to establish an academic reading habit for yourself.
iPaca is designed to get you start to get you into academic reading from the ground up. The only requirement is a solid command of basic English which was likely provided by your prior education.
